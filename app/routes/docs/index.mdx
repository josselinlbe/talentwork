# Introduction

## What is SaasRock?

A low-code & extensible framework with out-of-the-box SaaS features.

## Pre-launch

SaasRock is on pre-launch, meaning that the [license](/docs/license) will change, and the framework could have breaking changes until it reaches v1.0, check out the [changelog](/changelog) page to see the current version.

The [early-access price](https://alexandromg.gumroad.com/l/SaasFrontends-Remix/alpha-access) is $299 for the Individual licence (1 developer) and $6,598 for the Team license (10 developers), and you will get 1 year of private repository access to get updates.
