# Events and Webhooks

Use the Events to log essential data and use Webhooks to customize your **business logic** within internal or external webhooks.

[Click here](https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=YLw_6Lb0Bqg) to watch the launch video of this feature.

### Every event

- Is something **important** that happened within your SaaS.
- Is called inside a [Remix](https://remix.run) action.
- Has a DTO for **TypeScript** support.
- Can call 0, 1 or more **Webhook** Endpoints _(internal or external)_.

### Use cases:

- Custom **business logic** when something happens.
- Call a Webhooks by [Zapier](https://zapier.com/).
